About the project

I was brought in to design a major update of a back office web portal used by the company's clients to control customer-facing storefronts.

The goals for the update were:

– Completely overhaul portal's structure to address numerous navigation issues
– Modernize the overall look of the portal to be more in line with the planned new visual style
– Make the portal mobile-friendly

I was the sole designer on the team and worked on: Discovery, UX and UI design, Prototyping

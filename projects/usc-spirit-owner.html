About the project

USC is a Ukrainian manufacturer of weighbridges for heavy goods vehicles and rail.

Spirit Owner is the second piece in the Spirit ecosystem made for weighing station owners and staff not directly involved with operating the weighbridges.

The app was designed to give owners and management staff access to weighing logs of all their weighbridges, provide notifications for any weighbridge hardware or software issues, and alert them about fraud attempts.

I was the sole product designer on the project.

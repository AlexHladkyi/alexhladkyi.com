About the project

USC is a Ukrainian manufacturer of weighbridges for heavy goods vehicles and rail.

I was hired to design the client/customer-facing side of the new weighbridge ecosystem. This application was the first piece and would be primarily used by weighbridge operators at smaller or mobile weighing stations.

I was the sole product designer on the project.

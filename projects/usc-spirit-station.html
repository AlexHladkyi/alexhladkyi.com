About the project

USC is a Ukrainian manufacturer of weighbridges for heavy goods vehicles and rail.

I was hired to design the client-facing side of the new weighbridge software ecosystem. This application was the first piece of the ecosystem.

I was the sole designer on the project.

Spirit Station application

The client had several goals in mind for this project. 1. Reduce the amount of time weighbridge operators spend on data entry; 2. Act as a show piece for manufacturers weighbridges; 3. Introduce data logging to discourage operators from unauthorized use of the weighbridges.

The key graphics on the main and form screens were build using 3d assets.

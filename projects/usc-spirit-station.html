About the project

USC is a Ukrainian manufacturer of weighbridges for heavy goods vehicles and rail.

I was hired to design the client-facing side of the new weighbridge software ecosystem. This application was the first piece of the ecosystem.

I was the sole designer on the project.

Weighbridge views

The weighbridge views were designed as a full, dynamic 3d scene. This approach was chosen with two goals in mind. Firstly, visually and in real-time represent the states of various systems and sensors. And secondly, be a showcase piece for the client's planned automated weighbridge ecosystem.

Weighbridge platforms — CAD models by the client, optimized by me;
Generic vehicle models — sourced, optimized by me.

About the project

USC is a Ukrainian manufacturer of weighbridges for heavy goods vehicles and rail.

The Driver mobile app was a third piece of the ecosystem, alongside Spirit Station and Spirit Owner, targeted at drivers of heavy goods vehicles.

The designs below are for the 1.0 version of the app. The focus was limited to the following three areas: help drivers find Spirit-enabled weighing stations, speed up their weighing process, and provide access to their digitally signed weighing records.

I was the sole product designer on the project.

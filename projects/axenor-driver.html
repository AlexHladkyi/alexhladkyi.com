Mobile Driver app for Axenor

About the project

Axenor.ai is the safety and risk reduction platform for trucking fleets that integrates telematics, in‑cab signals, and operations data to drive coaching, incentives, and policy compliance.

I was approached by the client to design a companion mobile app for their service that would surface key performance indicators to the drivers of trucking fleets.

For v1 the focus was limited to:

– Showing fleet drivers their Safety Scores and events that contribute to it
– Helping drivers track their daily and weekly goals
– Showing them estimated monetary end of the week bonuses
– Adding light gamification elements like streaks, achievements and leaderboards

I was the sole designer on the project.

Android-specific adjustments

While proper Android support was a low priority for v1, I've made a number of quick android-specific changes to make the app not feel too alien to the platform.

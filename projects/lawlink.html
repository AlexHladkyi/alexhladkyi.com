Mobile app for LawLink

About the project

LawLink is a service that helps lawyers connect with their existing and potential customers.

I was approached by the client to design the v1.0 of their universal mobile app. The project consisted of multiple discovery sessions with the client, and later also with the dev team for certain critical features, then preliminary designs milestones and finalized designs handoff to the developers.

I was responsible for all design aspects of the project, except post-delivery design QA.

LawLink Mobile

Platform for legal services providers

Designed the MVP mobile app for LawLink, a legal tech platform connecting clients with legal professionals. Attorneys on Demand

National court appearance service

Led the mobile product design for Attorneys on Demand, translating a complex, national web-based court appearance service into an intuitive universal app. Functioning as the sole designer from user flows to high-fidelity UI, I structured a tabbed, bottom-action navigation tailored for high-reachability on the go. The solution streamlines real-time case management by introducing prominent case update indicators and persistent data caching, ensuring critical legal notes and document uploads are safely stored until completion.

Mobile Product Design, Legal Tech (LawTech), Web-to-Mobile Migration, Mobile-First UX/UI
